### [Blog 1 – Proactive Strategies to Improve Network Resilience and Ensure Business Continuity on AWS](3.1-Blog1/)
This blog discusses proactive strategies that organizations can adopt to strengthen network resilience and maintain business continuity on AWS in the event of cybersecurity incidents or disasters. It highlights best practices such as applying the NIST CSF and AWS Well-Architected Framework, implementing a multi-account strategy, using Infrastructure as Code (IaC), preparing recovery accounts in alternate Regions, and performing automated resilience testing to ensure end-to-end operational continuity.

### [Blog 2 – Designing Serverless Integration Patterns for LLMs](3.2-Blog2/)
This blog explores optimized serverless integration models for building generative AI applications with LLMs on AWS. It covers direct invocation of LLMs from Lambda, chaining prompts using Step Functions, running parallel inference workflows for improved performance, applying caching strategies to reduce cost and latency, and implementing error-handling patterns, model-selection considerations, and architectural best practices for scalable AI systems.

### [Blog 3 – The Importance of Encryption and How AWS Supports It](3.3-Blog3/)
This blog explains the critical role of encryption in defense-in-depth security and how AWS enables customers to encrypt data at rest, in transit, and in use. It covers the fundamentals of encryption, independent key management, FIPS 140 compliance, envelope encryption, and AWS services such as KMS, CloudHSM, Encryption SDK, and ACM. The blog also highlights modern approaches like end-to-end encryption (E2EE), cryptographic computing, and post-quantum cryptography (PQC) technologies such as ML-KEM and post-quantum s2n-tls.
